Monster Crossing

Welcome, let's play a game. Rules are simple:

  • Collect 5 keys to win, before losing your lives.
  • Beware of the monsters that roam this land, they'll take a life if you cross with one.
  • You can hide in forests from the monsters but don't stay too long because you never know what is lurking in there.
  • Use arrow keys to move.
